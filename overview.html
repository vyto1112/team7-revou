Overview
Our team conducted an analysis of the Bikes Sales Europe dataset to extract business questions and find solutions to them. We followed a series of steps, including identifying the root causes of the problems, setting objectives, analyzing the dataset, and creating visualizations to better understand the results of our analysis. The main focus of our analysis is on how to increase the revenue of a product in a European country with a specific age group. Team 7 Semarang
